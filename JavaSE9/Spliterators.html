13.5 Spliterators

JDK 8 added a new type of iterator called a spliterator that is defined by the Spliterator interface. A spliterator cycles through a sequence of elements and in this regard it is similar to the iterators. However, the techniques required to use it differ. Furthermore, it offers substantially more functionality than does either Iterator or ListIterator. Perhaps the most important aspect of Spliterator is its ability to provide support parallel iteration of portions of the sequence.
