13.5.4 Spliterator Subinterfaces

There are several nested subinterfaces of Spliterator designed for use with the primitive types double, int, and long. These are called

  • Spliterator.OfDouble,
  • Spliterator.OfInt, and
  • Spliterator.OfLong.
  • Spliterator.OfPrimitive (serves as a superinterface)
